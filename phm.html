Mission and Vission

The mission of pharmacy is to promote health and well-being by providing quality pharmaceutical care, ensuring the safe and effective use of medications, and fostering patient-centered services. Pharmacists are committed to supporting public health through expert advice, education, and optimizing drug therapies, while maintaining ethical practices and a focus on continuous improvement.

The vision of pharmacy is to be a leader in healthcare, advancing patient care through innovation, evidence-based practices, and technology integration. Pharmacists aim to be recognized as essential healthcare providers, contributing to improved health outcomes, disease prevention, and overall quality of life, serving as a trusted resource for communities worldwide.
